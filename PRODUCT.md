# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Polish families, groups, and small businesses seeking a simple, trustworthy rental of a nine-seat Renault Trafic around Wieruszów, Kępno, Ostrzeszów, Syców, Kluczbork, Wieluń, and Złoczew.

## Product Purpose

Present the vehicles, pricing, pickup area, and contact path clearly enough that visitors can confidently reserve or call without unnecessary friction.

## Brand Personality

Calm, automotive, dependable. Premium through proportion, typography, photography, and restraint rather than decorative effects.

## Anti-references

Generic rental templates, SaaS-style gradients, blue-drenched sections, stock-looking imagery, oversized icons, glossy effects, excessive animation, and visual clutter.

## Design Principles

- Make the vehicle the visual evidence.
- Keep prices and contact actions immediately understandable.
- Use white space and precise alignment to signal quality.
- Preserve local accessibility and approachable language.
- Make every interaction work equally well on mobile and desktop.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA, keyboard access, visible focus, meaningful alt text, reduced-motion support, readable contrast, and thumb-friendly mobile controls.
